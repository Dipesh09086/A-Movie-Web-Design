peaky Blinders is a British crime drama TV series about the Shelby crime family, a gang in Birmingham, England, in the aftermath of World War I. Led by the ambitious and cunning Thomas Shelby, the family expands its criminal empire through violence, extortion, and illegal activities, coming into conflict with rival gangs and other powerful groups. The show, loosely based on a real historical gang, uses fictional characters within a backdrop of real historical events to explore themes of family, power, and the changing social and political landscape of the interwar years.
