Money Heist is a Spanish crime drama series about a mysterious mastermind called "The Professor" who recruits a group of eight robbers to execute a complex heist. The first heist involves printing \(2.4\) billion euros in the Royal Mint of Spain with 67 hostages, lasting 11 days and resulting in the escape of most of the team with almost \(1\) billion euros. The series follows the group, including a love story between Tokyo and Rio, as they deal with internal conflicts, betrayals, and external pressure from the police and public, eventually leading to a second, more dangerous heist on the Bank of Spain to rescue a team member
