about movie:

Saiyaara is a 2025 Bollywood musical romance about Krish (Ahaan Panday), a struggling musician, and Vaani (Aneet Padda), a poet dealing with heartbreak after being left at the altar. Their paths cross when Krish discovers Vaani's poetry, leading to a musical collaboration that blossoms into love, as they find healing in each other, but their journey takes a tragic turn when Vaani is diagnosed with early-onset Alzheimer's, threatening to erase their memories.
