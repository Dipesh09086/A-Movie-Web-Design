about movie:

Tere Ishk Mein (In Your Love) tells the turbulent story of Shankar, a fiery student activist, and Mukti, a psychology scholar who uses him as a subject for her thesis on controlling aggression, leading to a complex, obsessive, and tragic romance that spans years, culminating in a dramatic reunion years later where a pregnant Mukti seeks Shankar's help to care for her child.
